Welcome to Silent Lens, a HTML5 game made for the Global Game Jam 2014.

Silent Lens is a two player game. You play as a deaf individual who must work with your blind friend to solve puzzles. Individually you won't have all the clues needed to complete the level. Communicate with your parter to descibe what you are seeing or hearing and solve the challenge together.

Use W A S D to move your character through the doors.
